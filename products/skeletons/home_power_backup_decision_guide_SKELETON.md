## Problem Statement

Home power backup is a confusing, high-stakes purchase. Homeowners who experienced a painful outage either overspend on a system they don't understand or delay buying and remain vulnerable. There's no independent, non-salesy guide that breaks down generator vs. solar+battery vs. whole-home backup in plain language with real cost ranges.

- **Portable Generator:** What it is, what it costs ($500–$3,000 installed), who it's for, pros/cons, fuel requirements
- **Home Standby Generator:** What it is, what it costs ($5,000–$15,000 installed), who it's for, pros/cons, natural gas vs. propane
- **Solar + Battery (eg. Tesla Powerwall, Franklin WH):** What it is, what it costs ($10,000–$25,000 after incentives), who it's for, pros/cons, installation timeline

- Cost vs. coverage matrix (table)

### Section 4: Installation Reality

### Section 5: Maintenance & Run Costs
- Generator fuel costs (realistic annual burn)
