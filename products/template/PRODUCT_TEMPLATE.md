## Breaking Through Plateaus

You've been doing everything right, but progress has stalled. Sound familiar?

**This is normal.** Plateaus are a sign that your body/mind/habits are adapting. Here's how to break through:

**1. Introduce a New Variable**
Change one element of your routine to shock your system back into progress.

**2. Rest and Recover**
Sometimes the best thing you can do is step away for a few days. Consistency matters, but so does intensity.

**3. Go Back to Basics**
You may have gotten sloppy on fundamentals. Revisit Module 1 and lock in the foundations.

**4. Measure Differently**
If the scale/mirror/performance isn't moving, track something else. Progress shows up in unexpected places.

**5. Get Accountability**
Isolated effort is hard. Find someone who will hold you to your commitments.
