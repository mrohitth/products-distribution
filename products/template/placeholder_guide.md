## CONTENT PLANNING WORKSHEET

Before filling in this template, answer these questions:

### 1. Target Audience
- Who is the ideal customer? `[Demographics, situation, mindset]`
- What do they desperately want? `[Specific outcome]`
- What do they fear most? `[Specific pain point]`
- What have they already tried? `[Failed solutions]`
- Why have their previous attempts failed? `[Root causes]`

### 2. Transformation Promise
- What is the BEFORE state? `[Where they are now]`
- What is the AFTER state? `[Where they want to be]`
- How long will it take to see results? `[Timeline]`
- What proof do you have? `[Testimonials, data, case studies]`

### 3. Content Structure
- How many modules/sections? `[Number]`
- What's the logical sequence? `[Foundation → Advanced]`
- What's the single most important concept? `[Core insight]`
- What do most people get wrong? `[Common mistake]`
- What bonus or edge case content? `[Special scenarios]`

### 4. Offer Architecture
- What is the free guide/drip content? `[This template]`
- What is the core paid product? `[Course name and price]`
- What is the premium 1:1 option? `[Coaching, consulting]`
- What is the upsell path? `[Free → Core → Premium]`

### 5. Credibility
- What 4 credentials establish authority? `[Credentials]`
- What 3 testimonials validate the method? `[Stories]`
- What results have you personally achieved? `[Your proof]`
- What specific numbers/stats add credibility? `[Data points]`
