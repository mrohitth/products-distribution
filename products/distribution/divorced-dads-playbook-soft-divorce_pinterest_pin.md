## Description
Most divorced dads don't realize their ex mentally filed the divorce months before the paperwork finished. She's moved forward. You're stuck in legal limbo. This creates a real problem: she has no urgency to push the process forward while you're waiting on her to engage.

The Soft Divorce Playbook shows you why this happens, how to communicate without destroying coparenting, when legal leverage helps vs. hurts, and how to keep your standing as a parent—not a visitor with supervised time.

Stop waiting for her to care. Learn what actually moves the process forward.
